About UniGo

UniGo is a smart and intuitive transport-finding web app designed to simplify commuting for students and daily travelers in Islamabad. Whether you're moving between universities or navigating key city centers, UniGo helps you pinpoint routes quickly and easily.

Our Mission

At UniGo, our mission is simple: to make transport route lookup effortless, fast, and user-friendly. By focusing on commonly traveled routes—such as between universities and major hubs like Blue Area, Centaurus Mall, and Peshawar Mor—we aim to streamline daily commutes for students, faculty, and commuters across Islamabad.
